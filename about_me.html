About Me




I am a 22 year old Yinzer fascinated by computer engineering. Just like many other engineers my age, Legos and K'nex were the first indication that my brain enjoyed the puzzle solving that comes with engineering. As I grew a little older, I was unscrewing radios, TV remotes, and DVD players just to see what was inside. Luckily enough I was able to put them all back together without mom or dad finding out! Now as a student, I am intrigued by embedded systems and communications.
